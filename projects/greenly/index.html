FAQ

Questions, answered

How do you vet products?

Each product goes through a 30-point review covering materials, packaging, supply chain and end-of-life recyclability.

Is shipping really carbon-neutral?

Yes. We measure and offset 100% of delivery emissions through verified reforestation and renewable-energy projects.

Can I return products?

Absolutely — 30-day returns on everything, and we reuse or recycle anything sent back.

How does the refill program work?

Order refills on a schedule and send back empty containers with our prepaid compostable mailers.
